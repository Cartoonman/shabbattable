About Us


Frijay's mission is to help couples and individuals find a Jewish home to celebrate Shabbat dinner.

Many Jewish people in metropolitan areas may be away from their families. They may have nowhere to go for shabbat dinner.

Frijay provides an easy way to search, find, and request a seat to enjoy a family Shabbat dinner.
